<p><strong>The Smart Garage Door Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wi-Fi-based</li><li>Bluetooth-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart garage door controllers are categorized into Wi-Fi-based and Bluetooth-based systems. Wi-Fi-based controllers connect to the internet, allowing remote access and control from anywhere via smartphone apps. They often integrate with smart home systems, enhancing convenience and security. In contrast, Bluetooth-based controllers operate within a limited range, facilitating local communication directly between the user's smartphone and the garage door opener. While offering ease of use, Bluetooth systems lack the extensive features and remote accessibility of their Wi-Fi counterparts.</p></p>
