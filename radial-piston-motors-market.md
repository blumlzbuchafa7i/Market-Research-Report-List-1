<p><p>Radial piston motors are devices that convert fluid pressure into rotational motion, categorized mainly into pneumatic and hydraulic types. Pneumatic radial piston motors utilize compressed air for operation, offering advantages like lightweight design and quick response, ideal for applications requiring high speed and efficiency. In contrast, hydraulic radial piston motors use hydraulic fluids, providing higher torque and power density, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications in construction and industrial machinery. Each type serves distinct operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
