<p><strong>The Quantum Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optically-pumped Magnetometers</li><li>Solid-state Quantum Magnetometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quantum magnetometer market primarily consists of two types: optically-pumped magnetometers and solid-state quantum magnetometers. Optically-pumped magnetometers utilize the principles of optical pumping to enhance sensitivity and measure low magnetic fields, making them ideal for geological and biomedical applications. In contrast, solid-state quantum magnetometers leverage quantum properties of solid materials, such as nitrogen-vacancy centers in diamonds, to achieve high-resolution magnetic field detection. Both types are essential in various applications, including navigation, industrial sensing, and scientific research.</p></p>
